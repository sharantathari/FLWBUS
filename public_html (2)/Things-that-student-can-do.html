Things that student can do| Edodwaja

June 19, 2021

In schools, students are taught all the basic requirements to make a living. The students are not given an opportunity to explore new things in the class as they are only confined to the books.

The only way they can explore their capabilities is through participating in science expos, but only few students are aware of what is a science expo and how it is conducted as their teachers do not encourage students to know about them and participate in those events. Therefore, not all the students can understand and learn the practical implementation of the subjects.

The only times when they can work practically is mostly during science expos, and science exhibitions. They also have cultural fests, annual days and many more events conducted in their schooling level which are the major events conducted in schools. If we encourage the students to participate in science exhibitions students will work effectively to showcase their science experiments in the science expos and can get a chance to learn something new and explore deep into technologies.
